About Getting Over It

Originally released in 2017. Getting Over It is the legendary rage game: you are a man in a cauldron climbing a mountain with only a hammer, and one mistake can undo an hour of progress. This game is part of our Skill Games collection. Difficulty: Very Hard.

How to Play Getting Over It

Controls: Mouse only: move the hammer in arcs to push, pull and hook yourself upward

Goal: Climb the mountain to the top. There are no checkpoints.

Tips & Strategy

  • Practice the 'pogo' — planting the hammer below you and launching upward.
  • Slow, deliberate swings beat panicked flailing after a slip.
  • Hook ledges gently; yanking the hammer throws you off.
  • Falls are part of the game — treat each one as route practice.
