About Among Us Multiplayer

Originally released in 2018. Among Us is the social deduction hit: finish tasks as a Crewmate and vote out the Impostors — or lie, sabotage and eliminate the crew as the Impostor yourself. This game is part of our Skill Games collection. Difficulty: Medium.

How to Play Among Us Multiplayer

Controls: WASD to move, E or Space to use, Q to kill as Impostor, mouse for tasks and voting

Goal: Crewmates win by completing all tasks or ejecting every Impostor; Impostors win by eliminating the crew.

Tips & Strategy

  • As crew, move in groups — solo players are easy targets.
  • Fake tasks convincingly as Impostor: stand still for a believable duration.
  • Use cams and admin table to track movement.
  • Report bodies and share locations; information wins meetings.
