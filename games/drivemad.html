About Drive Mad

Originally released in 2022. Drive Mad is a physics driving game where you nurse a fragile monster truck over collapsing bridges, see-saws and ramps without flipping it or losing the cargo. This game is part of our Driving Games collection. Difficulty: Medium.

How to Play Drive Mad

Controls: W or Up arrow to accelerate, S or Down arrow to reverse/brake

Goal: Reach the finish line in one piece across dozens of increasingly ridiculous obstacle levels.

Tips & Strategy

  • Gentle taps beat full throttle — most flips come from too much speed.
  • Let the suspension settle before attempting a jump.
  • Reverse and reposition instead of forcing a bad approach.
  • Watch the level intro: it usually hints at the gimmick.
