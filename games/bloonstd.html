About Bloons TD

Originally released in 2007. Bloons TD is the tower-defense classic: place dart monkeys, tack shooters and cannons along the path and pop every balloon before it escapes. This game is part of our Skill Games collection. Difficulty: Medium.

How to Play Bloons TD

Controls: Mouse: select and place towers, upgrade them between rounds

Goal: Stop every wave of Bloons from reaching the end of the track.

Tips & Strategy

  • Place towers where they cover the most path — bends are prime real estate.
  • One upgraded tower beats two basic ones at the same cost.
  • Lead Bloons need bombs or fire; plan for them before round 28.
  • Save cash for the big rounds instead of over-buying early.
