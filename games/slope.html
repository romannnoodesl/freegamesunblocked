About Slope

Originally released in 2014. Slope is a lightning-fast 3D endless runner: steer a neon ball down an endless slope packed with red blocks, moving walls and sudden drops while the speed keeps climbing. This game is part of our Skill Games collection. Difficulty: Hard.

How to Play Slope

Controls: Left/Right arrow keys or A/D to steer the ball

Goal: Roll as far as you can without falling off the edge or smashing into red obstacles. Your score is the distance you survive.

Tips & Strategy

  • Stay near the middle of the track so you have room to dodge in either direction.
  • Tap the keys instead of holding them — oversteering causes most falls.
  • Only take yellow speed-boost pads when you can see the track ahead.
  • At high speed, watch the horizon and react to obstacles before they get close.
