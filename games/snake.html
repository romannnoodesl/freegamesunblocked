About Snake

Originally released in 1998. Snake is the mobile classic: eat the food, grow longer, and don't run into the walls or your own ever-lengthening tail. This game is part of our Retro Games collection. Difficulty: Easy.

How to Play Snake

Controls: Arrow keys to change direction

Goal: Grow the snake as long as possible without crashing.

Tips & Strategy

  • Hug a coiling pattern along the edges when you get long.
  • Never box yourself into a space smaller than your length.
  • Plan two turns ahead once the tail gets big.
  • Speed increases with length, so tighten your turns early.
