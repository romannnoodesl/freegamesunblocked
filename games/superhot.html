About Superhot

Originally released in 2016. SUPERHOT is the shooter where time only moves when you do. Dodge bullets in slow motion, shatter red enemies and turn every level into an action-movie puzzle. This game is part of our Shooting Games collection. Difficulty: Medium.

How to Play Superhot

Controls: WASD to move, mouse to aim, click to shoot, right-click or F to throw/grab weapons

Goal: Clear every red enemy in each level. Time freezes when you stand still.

Tips & Strategy

  • Stand still to think — the whole game rewards planning over reflexes.
  • Throw empty guns at armed enemies to stun and disarm them.
  • Watch for off-screen spawns marked by the red flash.
  • Punches and thrown bottles save ammo and buy time.
