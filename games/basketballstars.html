About Basketball Stars

Originally released in 2019. Basketball Stars is a fast 1v1 streetball game with dunks, blocks, steals and three-pointers — outplay the CPU or a friend in quick matches. This game is part of our Skill Games collection. Difficulty: Medium.

How to Play Basketball Stars

Controls: Arrow keys to move, Z/X (or the on-screen keys) to shoot, steal, dash and block

Goal: Score more than your opponent before the clock runs out.

Tips & Strategy

  • Pump fake first — defenders jump early, leaving an open drive.
  • Time your jump for blocks at the peak of their shot.
  • Use dash to recover on defense after getting beaten.
  • Corner threes are the highest-percentage outside shot.
