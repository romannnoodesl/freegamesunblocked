About Fnaf 1

Originally released in 2014. Five Nights at Freddy's puts you in the night-guard chair: watch the cameras, manage limited power and keep the animatronics out of your office until 6 AM. This game is part of our Skill Games collection. Difficulty: Medium.

How to Play Fnaf 1

Controls: Mouse: click cameras, door and light buttons

Goal: Survive five nights. Doors and lights drain power — run out and you're defenseless.

Tips & Strategy

  • Power is the real enemy; use doors only when something is at them.
  • Check Pirate Cove (Foxy) regularly or he'll sprint at your door.
  • Listen for footsteps and breathing — audio tells you more than cameras.
  • Bonnie and Chica approach from different doors; track both.
