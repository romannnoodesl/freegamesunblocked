About 2048

Originally released in 2014. 2048 is the elegant number puzzle: slide tiles, merge matching numbers and chase the elusive 2048 tile on a 4x4 grid. This game is part of our Calm Games collection. Difficulty: Medium.

How to Play 2048

Controls: Arrow keys to slide all tiles in one direction

Goal: Merge tiles to reach 2048 — then keep going for a high score.

Tips & Strategy

  • Keep your biggest tile locked in one corner and never move it.
  • Build your second-biggest tile next to the corner tile.
  • Avoid moving up (or whichever direction frees the corner) unless forced.
  • Think in chains: 2-4-8-16 lined up merges beautifully.
