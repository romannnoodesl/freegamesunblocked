About Ovo

Originally released in 2020. OvO is a slick stickman parkour platformer built around momentum: run, jump, slide, wall-jump and dive through minimalist levels to reach the flag. This game is part of our Skill Games collection. Difficulty: Medium.

How to Play Ovo

Controls: Arrow keys to run, Up to jump, Down to slide or smash, Down mid-air to dive

Goal: Reach the flag at the end of every level. Faster finish times earn better ratings and unlock new skins.

Tips & Strategy

  • Slide right before a jump to launch a much longer slide-jump.
  • Diving mid-air (Down) converts height into horizontal speed.
  • Wall-jump by pressing jump as you touch the wall — chain them to climb shafts.
  • Coins are optional; learn the level first, then route a coin run.
