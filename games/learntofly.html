About Learn To Fly

Originally released in 2009. Learn to Fly is the penguin launch classic: slide off a ramp, adjust your flight, earn cash for distance and buy gliders, rockets and ramps to fly further. This game is part of our Calm Games collection. Difficulty: Easy.

How to Play Learn To Fly

Controls: Left/Right arrows to tilt, Space to fire rockets (once unlocked)

Goal: Fly further and higher each attempt, upgrading your gear until the penguin truly learns to fly.

Tips & Strategy

  • Upgrade the ramp and glider before rockets — glide efficiency wins early.
  • Keep a shallow angle for distance records.
  • Fire rockets at the apex of your glide for maximum range.
  • Complete the achievement challenges for bonus cash.
