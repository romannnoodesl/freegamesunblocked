About Cookie Clicker

Originally released in 2013. Cookie Clicker is the original idle game: click a giant cookie, then spend cookies on cursors, grandmas and factories that bake for you around the clock. This game is part of our Calm Games collection. Difficulty: Easy.

How to Play Cookie Clicker

Controls: Mouse: click the big cookie, click buildings and upgrades to buy them

Goal: Bake as many cookies as possible and build an ever-growing automated cookie empire.

Tips & Strategy

  • Early on, buy the cheapest building each time — CpS scales faster that way.
  • Always click golden cookies; their bonuses stack with everything.
  • Buy 'double CpS' upgrades as soon as they appear.
  • Leave the tab open — idle production does most of the work.
