About Eggy Car

Originally released in 2019. Eggy Car puts a raw egg on your roof and dares you to drive over hills without letting it fall and crack. Simple controls, brutal physics. This game is part of our Driving Games collection. Difficulty: Hard.

How to Play Eggy Car

Controls: Right arrow/D to accelerate, Left arrow/A to brake or reverse

Goal: Drive as far as possible while keeping the egg on the car. Distance is your score.

Tips & Strategy

  • Tiny throttle taps keep the egg settled; speed is the enemy.
  • Slow down before every hill crest — that's where eggs launch.
  • If the egg starts bouncing, stop completely and let it settle.
  • Grab power-ups like the egg freeze when they appear.
