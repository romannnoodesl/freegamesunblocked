About Cluster Rush

Originally released in 2016. Cluster Rush is first-person truck-surfing: sprint and leap across the roofs of speeding trucks in a convoy and don't touch the ground. This game is part of our Skill Games collection. Difficulty: Hard.

How to Play Cluster Rush

Controls: WASD or arrows to move, Space to jump

Goal: Jump from truck to truck and reach the finish without falling or getting thrown.

Tips & Strategy

  • Jump early — the trucks are moving faster than your reflexes.
  • Land on cab centers; edges throw you off.
  • Keep moving forward; standing still gets you crushed by the next truck.
  • Watch for turning trucks — they tilt before they swerve.
