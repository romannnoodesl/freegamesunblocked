About Bloxorz

Originally released in 2007. Bloxorz is a spatial puzzle classic: roll a 1x1x2 block across floating platforms and drop it upright into the square hole without tumbling off the edge. This game is part of our Calm Games collection. Difficulty: Hard.

How to Play Bloxorz

Controls: Arrow keys to roll the block one space at a time

Goal: Stand the block upright on the exit hole across 33 levels of switches, bridges and fragile tiles.

Tips & Strategy

  • Remember the block covers two tiles when lying down — most falls come from forgetting that.
  • Plan the last two moves first: the block must arrive upright.
  • X-switches need the block standing; O-switches work either way.
  • Orange tiles break if the block stands on them — cross them lying down.
