About Run 3

Originally released in 2014. Run 3 sends a little alien sprinting through crumbling space tunnels where you can rotate the entire tunnel to run on walls and ceilings. This game is part of our Skill Games collection. Difficulty: Medium.

How to Play Run 3

Controls: Left/Right arrows (or A/D) to move, Up or Space to jump; touching a wall rotates the tunnel

Goal: Get through each tunnel without falling into space. Explore mode unlocks new characters; Infinite mode tests your endurance.

Tips & Strategy

  • Look two tiles ahead — crumbling tiles give almost no warning.
  • Rotate early: running onto a wall mid-jump changes your landing completely.
  • Unlock and try different characters; the Skater handles ice far better.
  • In Infinite mode, play safe routes instead of chasing cells.
