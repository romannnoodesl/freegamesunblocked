About 1V1.LOL

Originally released in 2019. 1v1.LOL is a build-and-fight shooter where you throw up walls, ramps and floors mid-battle. Play 1v1 duels, box fights or battle royale against real players. This game is part of our Shooting Games collection. Difficulty: Hard.

How to Play 1V1.LOL

Controls: WASD to move, mouse to aim/shoot, Z/X/C/V to build walls, ramps, floors and roofs, Space to jump

Goal: Eliminate your opponent before they eliminate you. Win rounds by out-building and out-aiming the other player.

Tips & Strategy

  • Learn to place a ramp with a wall in front of it — that one move wins most fights.
  • Take and hold high ground; fighting downward is always easier.
  • Practice editing builds in the free build mode before queueing.
  • Don't spam builds — each piece has a cooldown that good players punish.
