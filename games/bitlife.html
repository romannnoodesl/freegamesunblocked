Originally released in 2018. BitLife is a life simulator: make choices from birth to death — school, careers, relationships, lottery tickets and mischief — and see how the story ends. This game is part of our Skill Games collection. Difficulty: Easy.

  • Keep Smarts and Health high with the library and gym each year.
  • Start saving early — compound money buys houses and businesses later.
