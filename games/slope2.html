Originally released in 2022. Slope 2 keeps the same high-speed downhill ball rolling as the original and adds moving obstacles, twisting tunnels and brand-new tracks. This game is part of our Skill Games collection. Difficulty: Hard.

How to Play Slope 2

Controls: Left/Right arrow keys or A/D to steer

Goal: Survive as far as possible down faster, trickier slopes while dodging red blocks and gaps.

Tips & Strategy

  • The tunnels hide obstacles — enter them centered and straight.
  • Moving red blocks have a rhythm; watch one full cycle before committing.
  • Short taps beat long holds at max speed.
  • Don't chase gems into risky lines — survival scores more.
