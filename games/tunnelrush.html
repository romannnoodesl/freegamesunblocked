About Tunnel Rush

Originally released in 2018. Tunnel Rush hurls you through a spinning kaleidoscope tunnel full of barriers. Dodge at speed as the colors blur and the pace keeps rising. This game is part of our Calm Games collection. Difficulty: Hard.

How to Play Tunnel Rush

Controls: Left/Right arrows or A/D to rotate around the tunnel

Goal: Survive the tunnel as long as possible, slipping through gaps in the oncoming barriers.

Tips & Strategy

  • Focus on the center vanishing point — obstacles read faster there.
  • Small movements only; big spins make you lose orientation.
  • Anticipate the tunnel's rotation instead of reacting to it.
  • If colors distract you, defocus your eyes and track the gaps.
