About Flappy Bird

Originally released in 2013. Flappy Bird is the one-button phenomenon: flap a bird through gaps in green pipes. One pixel of contact ends the run — and your patience. This game is part of our Skill Games collection. Difficulty: Hard.

How to Play Flappy Bird

Controls: Click, tap or Space to flap upward

Goal: Fly through as many pipe gaps as possible without touching anything.

Tips & Strategy

  • Find a steady flap rhythm and hold it instead of reacting late.
  • Stay in the lower-middle of the screen for the best angles.
  • Flap before the gap, not inside it.
  • Take breaks — tilt makes this game genuinely harder.
