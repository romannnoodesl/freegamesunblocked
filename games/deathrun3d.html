About Death Run 3D

Originally released in 2015. Death Run 3D is a first-person reflex gauntlet: fly through neon tunnels at extreme speed and thread the gaps in walls that give you milliseconds to react. This game is part of our Skill Games collection. Difficulty: Very Hard.

How to Play Death Run 3D

Controls: WASD or arrow keys to move within the tunnel

Goal: Survive each course as long as possible at blistering speed.

Tips & Strategy

  • Look at the far end of the tunnel, not the wall in front of you.
  • Move in tiny corrections — at full speed a small input crosses the whole screen.
  • Learn each course's patterns; they're fixed, not random.
  • Turn off distractions — this game is 90% focus.
