About Monkey Mart

Originally released in 2022. Monkey Mart is a cute management game where your monkey runs a supermarket: plant crops, stock shelves, ring up customers and expand the store. This game is part of our Calm Games collection. Difficulty: Easy.

How to Play Monkey Mart

Controls: WASD or arrow keys to move — your monkey works automatically when standing at a station

Goal: Grow the mart by keeping shelves stocked and customers happy, unlocking new aisles and products.

Tips & Strategy

  • Never let bananas run out — they're the fastest seller.
  • Hire helpers as soon as you can afford them; automation scales.
  • Upgrade movement and carry speed early.
  • Save for the next aisle unlock before buying decorations.
