About Stickman Hook

Originally released in 2018. Stickman Hook is a one-button swinging game: latch onto hooks, build momentum through the air and fling your stickman to the finish line. This game is part of our Skill Games collection. Difficulty: Medium.

How to Play Stickman Hook

Controls: Hold Space or mouse button to grapple, release to fly

Goal: Swing through every level and cross the finish line without falling.

Tips & Strategy

  • Release at the forward peak of the swing for maximum distance.
  • You don't need every hook — skipping one is often faster.
  • Bounce pads preserve momentum; land on them when possible.
  • Short taps give fine control on tight sections.
