About Level Devil

Originally released in 2023. Level Devil is a troll platformer where the level itself lies to you: floors vanish, spikes sprout and the exit door runs away. Trust nothing. This game is part of our Skill Games collection. Difficulty: Hard.

How to Play Level Devil

Controls: Arrow keys or WASD to move, Space or Up to jump

Goal: Reach the door in each level while the game invents new ways to betray you.

Tips & Strategy

  • Expect a trap on literally every step of a first attempt.
  • Move slowly and let traps trigger before you commit.
  • Memorize trap locations — each level is short and restartable.
  • Sometimes the 'obvious' path is the only real one; sometimes it isn't.
