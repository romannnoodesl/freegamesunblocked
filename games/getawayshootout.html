About Getaway Shootout

Originally released in 2018. Getaway Shootout is a chaotic ragdoll race: hop your wobbly character across rooftops and tracks, grab guns and power-ups, and reach the getaway vehicle first. This game is part of our Shooting Games collection. Difficulty: Medium.

How to Play Getaway Shootout

Controls: W and E to hop left and right, R to use your held weapon or power-up

Goal: Beat three rivals to the helicopter, car or train at the end of each map.

Tips & Strategy

  • Master the hop rhythm — alternating W/E fast is quicker than big jumps.
  • Grab any weapon early; knockback wins close races.
  • Save your power-up for the final stretch to the vehicle.
  • Knocking rivals off high platforms sets them back further than shooting them.
