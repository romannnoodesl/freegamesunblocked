About Retro Bowl

Originally released in 2020. Retro Bowl mixes arcade football with team management: call the shots on offense, then handle drafts, salaries and morale in a pixel-art front office. This game is part of our Skill Games collection. Difficulty: Medium.

How to Play Retro Bowl

Controls: Click and drag to aim a pass, release to throw; arrow keys or WASD to dodge defenders after the catch

Goal: Win games, make the playoffs and lift the Retro Bowl while keeping your roster under the salary cap.

Tips & Strategy

  • Upgrade your QB first — passing wins games at every difficulty.
  • A fast wide receiver beats two slow ones; spend on one star.
  • Run the clock when ahead instead of forcing deep passes.
  • Trading aging players before they decline keeps the cap healthy.
