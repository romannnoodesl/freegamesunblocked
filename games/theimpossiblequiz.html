About The Impossible Quiz

Originally released in 2007. The Impossible Quiz is 110 questions of pure trickery where the obvious answer is always wrong. Think sideways, expect puns and guard your three lives. This game is part of our Calm Games collection. Difficulty: Hard.

How to Play The Impossible Quiz

Controls: Mouse to click answers — and sometimes the question itself

Goal: Answer every question without losing all three lives. Skips help, but you need them all for the end.

Tips & Strategy

  • Read questions literally — wordplay beats logic here.
  • Some answers are hidden in the question text or the screen itself.
  • Memorize answers after each failure; progress is pattern knowledge.
