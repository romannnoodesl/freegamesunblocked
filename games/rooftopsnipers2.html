About Rooftop Snipers 2

Originally released in 2020. Rooftop Snipers 2 is a two-button physics duel: jump and shoot to knock your opponent off the roof with pistols, tomahawks and beach balls. This game is part of our Shooting Games collection. Difficulty: Medium.

How to Play Rooftop Snipers 2

Controls: W to jump, E to shoot (Player 2 uses arrow keys/O)

Goal: Win rounds by knocking the other sniper off the rooftop — first to the round target wins.

Tips & Strategy

  • Shoot while your opponent is mid-jump; they can't dodge.
  • High arcs get more knockback than flat shots.
  • Watch for stage hazards like wind and moving platforms.
  • Jumping changes your hitbox — mix it up when you're losing duels.
