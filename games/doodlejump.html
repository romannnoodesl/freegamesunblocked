About Doodle Jump

Originally released in 2009. Doodle Jump is the classic vertical climber: bounce from platform to platform up an endless notebook page, riding springs and dodging monsters. This game is part of our Skill Games collection. Difficulty: Medium.

How to Play Doodle Jump

Controls: Left/Right arrows to move; you bounce automatically

Goal: Climb as high as possible without falling off the bottom of the screen.

Tips & Strategy

  • Aim for springs and jetpacks — they're free altitude.
  • You wrap around screen edges; use it to reach far platforms.
  • Brown platforms break — land and leave immediately.
  • Shoot monsters (Up or Space) before landing near them.
