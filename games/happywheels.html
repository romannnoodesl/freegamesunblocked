About Happy Wheels

Originally released in 2010. Happy Wheels is the infamous ragdoll physics game: guide Segway riders, wheelchair grandpas and irresponsible dads through lethally silly obstacle courses. This game is part of our Skill Games collection. Difficulty: Hard.

How to Play Happy Wheels

Controls: Arrow keys to move, Space for the primary action, Shift/Ctrl for secondary actions

Goal: Reach the finish line in one piece — or at least with the pieces you need.

Tips & Strategy

  • Slow is safe: most traps punish full speed.
  • Lean back on descents to avoid face-planting.
  • Eject (Z) before a bad landing to sometimes survive it.
  • Different characters trivialize different levels — swap around.
