About Geometry Dash

Originally released in 2013. Geometry Dash is a one-button rhythm platformer where you jump, fly and flip through spike-filled levels synced to pounding soundtracks. This game is part of our Skill Games collection. Difficulty: Hard.

How to Play Geometry Dash

Controls: Space, Up arrow or mouse click to jump; hold to keep jumping/flying

Goal: Reach the end of each level without hitting spikes or walls — one mistake restarts the run.

Tips & Strategy

  • Use practice mode to place checkpoints and learn the layout section by section.
  • Tap to the beat — obstacle timing matches the music.
  • Don't watch your icon; watch the obstacles ahead of it.
  • Short controlled taps beat panic-holding on ship sections.
