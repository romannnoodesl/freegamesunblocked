About Fireboy and Watergirl

Originally released in 2009. Fireboy and Watergirl is the co-op puzzle classic: guide two characters through temple levels using their opposite elemental powers — alone or with a friend on one keyboard. This game is part of our Skill Games collection. Difficulty: Medium.

How to Play Fireboy and Watergirl

Controls: Arrow keys for Fireboy, WASD for Watergirl

Goal: Get both characters to their matching doors, collecting diamonds. Fire hurts Watergirl, water hurts Fireboy, and green goo hurts both.

Tips & Strategy

  • Move one character at a time on tricky sections; park the other somewhere safe.
  • Use one character to hold levers and buttons for the other.
  • Grab the red diamonds with Fireboy and blue ones with Watergirl.
  • Perfect runs need every diamond — scout the level first.
