About Crossy Road

Originally released in 2014. Crossy Road is endless Frogger: hop across highways, rivers and train tracks one tap at a time while traffic, trains and eagles try to end the run. This game is part of our Skill Games collection. Difficulty: Medium.

How to Play Crossy Road

Controls: Arrow keys or click to hop forward; Left/Right to dodge sideways

Goal: Hop as far as you can. Standing still too long summons the eagle.

Tips & Strategy

  • Never stop moving forward for long — the eagle is always coming.
  • Sideways hops dodge trains without losing ground.
  • Ride logs and lily pads at their rhythm, not yours.
  • Listen for train horns; they warn you before you can see the train.
