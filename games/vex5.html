About Vex 5

Originally released in 2020. Vex 5 is a precision stickman platformer packed with buzzsaws, spikes and disappearing blocks. Run, slide and wall-jump through deadly acts to the exit. This game is part of our Skill Games collection. Difficulty: Hard.

How to Play Vex 5

Controls: WASD or arrows to run and jump, S or Down to slide

Goal: Reach the end of each act alive. Checkpoints save your progress within a level.

Tips & Strategy

  • Slide under saws instead of jumping them when the ceiling is low.
  • Chain wall-jumps with momentum — hesitation drops you into spikes.
  • Purple blocks vanish and orange blocks bounce; learn their timing.
  • Touch every checkpoint, even out of the way ones.
